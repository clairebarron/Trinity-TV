Hello welcome to my page..... This course is aimed at those wanting a comprehensive knowledge of the contemporary EU landscape. We will explore the impact of the EU on a range of policy areas including, economic, competition, social and foreign policies. The course will also focus on the EU as a locus for political representation exploring how well the EU functions as a democratic institution and how citizens engage with it. In particular, the course will examine these issues in light of contemporary challenges including the economic crisis, the refugee crisis, Brexit and the rise of populist politics.
